  • 2022 - Present
    Assistant Attending (Faculty)
    Memorial Sloan Kettering, Department of Medical Physics

I design, develop, and implement efficient algorithms for large-scale, distributed optimization and machine learning with applications to IMRT, IMPT, VMAT, and other treatment modalities.
